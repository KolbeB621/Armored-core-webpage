This is five chapter 2 armored core builds

  1. Pulse Pressure Build-Stagger-focused, laser-heavy build that melts defenses once the shield drops.
  2. Grenadier Breaker-Explosive build made to obliterate enemy groups and stagger tougher targets with one shot.
  3. Airborne Spearhead-Hovering menace that dives in, hits hard, and escapes fast.
  4. Missile Swarm Specialist-Why aim when the missiles do it for you? Lock, launch, reload, repeat.
  5. Juggernaut Bunker-Get close, get dirty, land the bunker, walk away.

Pulse Pressure Build

This AC uses pulse guns and laser weaponry to break enemy posture quickly. Once enemies are staggered, it finishes them off with high DPS shoulder weapons. The build uses midweight bipedal legs for mobility and energy management. Great against shielded and armored targets.

  • Strengths:Great vs. energy-shielded enemies, good balance of range and DPS.
  • Weaknesses:EN-hungry; struggles with EN disruption.
  • Best for:Chapter 2 missions with heavier MT enemies and AC duels.

  • Booster-ALULA 21E

Grenadier Breaker

Explosive build made to obliterate enemy groups and stagger tougher targets with one shot.

  • Strengths:Burst kills, great stagger synergy.
  • Weaknesses:Slow reloads, heavy weight.
  • Best for:Area denial, anti-AC, base destruction missions.

Airborne Spearhead

Hovering menace that dives in, hits hard, and escapes fast.

  • Strengths:Elevation abuse, great melee follow-ups.
  • Weaknesses:Fragile; weak to snipers or flak.

Missile Swarm Specialist

Why aim when the missiles do it for you? Lock, launch, reload, repeat.

  • Strengths:Minimal aiming, constant pressure, excellent vs. groups.
  • Weaknesses:Low direct DPS; weak vs. single high-mobility ACs.
