This is five quick and easy starter builds

  1. Balanced Assault– All-purpose AC for early missions; good at all ranges with reliable gear.
  2. Close-Range Brawler– Aggressive melee build with shotguns and a blade for stagger + burst.
  3. Heavy Tank– Slow but deadly tank with massive firepower and durability.
  4. Long-Range Artillery– Sniper-style AC using linear rifles and heavy shoulder weapons from afar.
  5. Hit-and-Run Speedster– High-speed, light AC built for evasion and quick burst attacks.

1. Balanced Assault Build

This is the perfect starting build. It mixes ballistic and energy weapons for flexibility, and uses bipedal legs for balance between speed and load. Missile launchers add range and stagger support, while a medium-tier FCS and generator keep things stable. It won’t outshine in any one area, but you can win most missions without tweaking much.

  • Strengths: Adaptable, ammo-efficient, decent mobility.
  • Weaknesses: No strong specialization.
  • Best For: First-time players or general-purpose mission clears.

2. Close-Range Brawler

This AC is all about closing distance quickly and punishing with high-impact weapons like shotguns and a laser blade. Reverse-joint legs give it great mobility and jump height, ideal for dodging and lunging at enemies. This is a high-risk/high-reward build—great against enemy ACs or bosses when you control the tempo.

  • Strengths: Burst damage, high stagger, very agile.
  • Weaknesses: Fragile at range, relies on skillful movement.
  • Best For: Players who like pressure and close combat tactics.

3. Heavy Tank Build

The heavy tank is the go-to siege platform. With a Gatling gun and pile bunker for up close and heavy missiles plus SONGBIRDS for AoE destruction, it wrecks anything in front of it. You’ll need to get used to slow movement and ground-only navigation (tank legs), but in raw power, it’s unmatched early on.

  • Strengths: Massive damage, huge durability.
  • Weaknesses: Low mobility, can't hover or dodge well.
  • Best For: Defense missions, boss trades, or stationary sieges.

4. Long-Range Sniper/Artillery Build

Quad legs allow hovering for stable sniping, and this build takes full advantage of it. The linear rifle is accurate and efficient, while back-mounted laser and missile systems keep pressure on targets. You’ll need positioning awareness but can stay out of danger and chip down tougher enemies with minimal risk.

  • Strengths: Long-range control, hover mobility, strong sustained fire.
  • Weaknesses: Weak at close range, requires aim and timing.
  • Best For: Missions with open terrain or boss sniping.

5. Hit-and-Run Speed Build

Built for mobility and evasion, this lightweight AC zips around the field using burst machine weapons and multi-missile locks. It’s excellent for scouting, fast clears, and exploiting openings. You’ll sacrifice durability, so success depends on dodging and decision-making, not brute strength.

  • Strengths: Ultra-mobile, fast lock-ons, great burst damage.
  • Weaknesses: Fragile, energy-dependent.
  • Best For: Skilled players who want fast-paced combat.
